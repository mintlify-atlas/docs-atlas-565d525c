Silent Gear transforms how you craft tools, weapons, and armor in Minecraft. Every piece of gear is assembled from individual parts — each made from a material you choose — giving you complete control over performance, aesthetics, and special abilities called traits. Materials and parts are fully customizable through data packs, and add-on mods can extend the system with new gear types, part types, and trait effects.

## What makes Silent Gear different

Silent Gear replaces the standard Minecraft crafting recipes for tools and armor with a **blueprint-based system** that eliminates recipe conflicts. You craft parts first, then combine them using blueprints at a crafting table or gear smithing table.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the mod">
    Download Silent Gear from [CurseForge](https://minecraft.curseforge.com/projects/silent-gear) or [Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/mod/silent-gear) and add it to your NeoForge instance along with Silent Lib.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Obtain blueprints">
    When you first join a world, you'll receive a blueprint package. Right-click it to unpack starter blueprints for making common gear parts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Gather materials and craft parts">
    Use your blueprints with raw materials at a crafting table to make parts. The main part determines the gear type; other parts add stats and traits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assemble your gear">
    Combine the parts in a crafting grid with a gear blueprint to produce your finished tool or weapon. Check the Material Book for stats on every material.
  </Step>
</Steps>
